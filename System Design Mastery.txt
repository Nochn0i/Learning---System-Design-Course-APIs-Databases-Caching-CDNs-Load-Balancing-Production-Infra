Choosing the right Database
Two main options
1. Relational Database
2. Non-Relational Database (NoSQL database)

- Relational Databases
    - Uses SQL language
    - Tables are used
    -Advantages
        - Support complex join operations
        - Data consistency and integrity
        - Transaction -> ACID
            - Atomicity : completely successds or completely fails.
            - Consistency: valid => another valid state.
            - Isolation: isolated from other trnsactions.
            - Durability: data remains in case of system failure.

- Non-Relational Database
    . MongoDB   = document stores
    . Kasandra  = wide-column stores
    . Reddis    = key-value stores
    . Neo4G     = Graph

- Relational Vs Non-Relational

    > Relational
        . Data is well structured with clear relationships.
        . Strong consistency and transactional integrity.

    > Non-Relational
        . Super-low latency for rapid response.
        . Data is unstructured or semi-structured.
        . Scalable storage for massive data volumes.
